# BlackBelt v2 — Business Model

## Platform Tiers

### Free
- Up to 30 students
- 1 unit
- Basic check-in (QR code)
- Basic reports
- Community support
- BlackBelt branding

### Pro — R$ 199/month
- Up to 300 students
- Up to 3 units
- Advanced check-in (QR + proximity)
- Full analytics and insights
- Notification hub (email + WhatsApp)
- Automations (up to 10 rules)
- Custom branding (logo + colors)
- API access (1,000 req/day)
- Priority support

### Enterprise — R$ 499/month
- Unlimited students
- Unlimited units
- All Pro features
- SSO (SAML/OIDC)
- Audit log
- Franchise management
- Network dashboard
- Full API access (unlimited)
- Dedicated support
- SLA 99.9%
- Custom integrations

## Marketplace Revenue

### App Store Commissions
- Free apps: no commission
- Paid apps: 20% commission on recurring revenue
- One-time purchases: 15% commission

### Plugin Developer Revenue Share
- Developer receives 80% of recurring revenue
- Developer receives 85% of one-time sales
- Monthly payout via PIX or bank transfer
- Minimum payout: R$ 50

## Additional Revenue Streams

### Marketplace Courses
- Course creators publish on the marketplace
- Platform takes 25% commission
- Creator receives 75%

### Physical Store
- Academy-branded products sold through platform store
- Platform takes 10% fulfillment fee
- Shipping handled by academy or integrated logistics

### Certifications
- Verified digital certificates via blockchain
- R$ 5 per certificate issued (included in Pro/Enterprise)
- Public verification page

### Championships
- Platform hosts championship management
- R$ 2 per participant for registration processing
- Free for Enterprise tier

## Key Assumptions

- Average academy size: 100 students
- Pro conversion rate: 30% of free users
- Enterprise conversion rate: 5% of Pro users
- App Store attach rate: 2.5 paid apps per Pro academy
- Churn rate target: < 5% monthly
