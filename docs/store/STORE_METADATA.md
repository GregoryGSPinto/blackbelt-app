4. Students — Student roster, profile details, and belt progression history
5. Belt Graduation — Requirements per belt, exam history, progression tracking
6. Finance — Invoices, payment tracking, delinquency reports (admin only)
7. Video Lessons — Instructional video library hosted on Bunny Stream CDN. Videos are created and uploaded by academy instructors, not by BlackBelt
8. Messaging — Communication between professors, students, and guardians

SUGGESTED TEST FLOW:
1. Open app, login with admin credentials above
2. Explore Dashboard — see KPIs and charts
3. Go to Classes — view weekly schedule
4. Go to Students — browse roster, tap a student to see belt history
5. Tap check-in button (red FAB) — select "Manual Check-in" — confirm
6. Go to Finance — view invoices and payment status
7. Go to Video Lessons — browse library, play a video
8. Log out, log in as student (aluno@demo.blackbelt.app / Demo@2026!)
9. As student: check-in, view belt progress, watch video lessons

PAYMENTS — IMPORTANT:
This app does NOT process payments in-app and does NOT require In-App Purchase. BlackBelt is a B2B SaaS tool — academy owners (businesses, not individual consumers) subscribe through our website. The payment gateway is Asaas (Brazilian B2B payment processor), handled entirely outside the app. Financial features visible in the app are management/reporting tools for academy owners to track their external invoices and student payments. This is identical to how Mindbody, Glofox, Zen Planner, and Wodify operate in the App Store.

CHILDREN'S DATA:
The app includes Kids (under 13) and Teen (13-17) profiles, always managed by parent/guardian accounts. Minor data handling complies with LGPD (Brazilian GDPR) Article 14 and follows COPPA principles:

- Kids have NO ability to create or share content
- Kids see a simplified, restricted UI
- Registration requires active parental consent
- See our children's privacy policy: blackbeltv2.vercel.app/privacidade-menores

TECHNICAL NOTES:
- The app requires an internet connection (online-first architecture)
- Push notifications require a physical device
- Backend is active 24/7 at https://blackbeltv2.vercel.app
- No advertising is displayed anywhere in the app
- Camera permission is used for QR Code check-in scanning (optional feature)
